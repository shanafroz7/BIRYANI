FACTS ABOUT BIRYANI.

  1. Biryani is a famous Indian rice dish.
  2. The word Biryani comes from the Persian language.
  3. It is made with rice, spices, and meat or vegetables.
  4. Hyderabadi Biryani is the most popular type.
  5. It is often served with raita or salad.
  6. Biryani can be veg or non-veg.Mine favourite is non-veg.
  7. It is a complete meal by itself.
  8. Biryani is enjoyed by people of all ages.
  9. Biryani is famous for its long rice grains.
  10. It is one of the most loved dishes in India.
